The Shawshank Redemption
Actors Quotes

Summary

Andy Dufresne, a banker convicted of murdering his wife and her lover, is sentenced to life in Shawshank Prison. Over the years, he forms an unlikely friendship with fellow inmate Red, and through his intelligence and hope, he finds ways to cope with the harsh realities of prison life, ultimately seeking redemption.

Actors

Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne: A quiet, intelligent banker who adapts to prison life with resilience.

Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red" Redding: A long-term inmate who narrates the story and befriends Andy.

Bob Gunton as Warden Norton: The strict and corrupt warden of Shawshank Prison.

Favorite Quote
