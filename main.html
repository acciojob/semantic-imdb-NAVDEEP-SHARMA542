The story follows Angad, a carefree young man from a wealthy family, who meets Megha, a beautiful and enigmatic woman, at a wedding. They fall in love, but Megha's past—a traumatic incident involving her ex-boyfriend—creates obstacles. Angad's obsession with Megha leads to a series of events that test their relationship, including misunderstandings, family interventions, and revelations about Megha's history. The film delves into the darker side of love, exploring how past traumas can haunt relationships. Without spoiling the ending, it culminates in themes of forgiveness and moving forward.
